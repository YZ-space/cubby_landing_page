ABOUT CUBBY
What is Cubby?

We built an interactive learning management system, targeting students ages 5-7. In light of the pandemic in 2020, the issue of virtual learning in young children became more prominent.

Our solution aims to facilitate social-emotional learning, and facilitate parent-teacher relationships to best benefit each student in their learning experience.

Our key features
What makes Cubby different than other platforms?
Facilitating communication

Our platform aims to foster communication between teachers, parents, and students alike. We focused Cubby’s experience to be one that considers every aspect of a virtual learning environment.

Immersive learning experience

With our solution targeting young children, we want to create an environment that fosters an immersive learning experience for a well-rounded curriculum, accomodating for various teaching methods and approaches.

Personalized feedback system

At this age, students require a more specific way to receive feedback. We’ve developed a customized sticker system that reinforces social-emotional learning beyond academics, fostering cognitive development.
